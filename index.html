Our Solution

Investartups.up – Simplifying Startup-Investor Connections

We created Investartups.up as a “platform as a service” to address these challenges with an AI-powered platform that matches investors and startups based on mutual interests, industries, and investment criteria. Startups can present their ideas through engaging short video pitches, while investors can quickly filter and connect with startups that align with their strategy.

Our mission

Our mission is to make the entire process faster, more transparent, and efficient for both sides.

How It Works

Step 1: AI-Powered Matching

Both startups and investors create profiles detailing their focus areas, needs, and criteria.

Our advanced AI technology intelligently matches investors with relevant startups based on these inputs.

Step 2: Video Pitches

Startups upload short, dynamic video pitches, making it easier for investors to understand the business and its potential.

Step 3: Business Dating – Match & Connect

Startups can request calls with investors through our “Business Dating” feature. Once the investor accepts, both sides can start meaningful discussions to explore potential investments.
